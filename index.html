Status of Electrification

BILECO has been adamant in fulfilling its mandate on total electrification within its franchise area. Since the start of its operation in 1983, BILECO made significant turns which eventually changed the landscape of electrification in Biliran. Energizing the municipalities and barangays one after the other were milestones that inspired us to continue to march until the last remaining household is energized.

With its continued commitment to missionary electrification, BILECO has energized more than 92% of the total potential sitios in its area coverage and 94% of its potential house connections.
